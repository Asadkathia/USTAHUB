Frequently Asked Questions

Is it free to create an account?

Yes! Creating an account on UstaHub is completely free for both customers and service providers. You only pay when you book a service or receive payment for completed work.

How do you verify service providers?

We conduct thorough background checks, verify licenses and insurance, check references, and review qualifications to ensure all providers meet our high standards.

How does payment work?

Customers pay securely through our platform after service completion. Service providers receive payments within 24-48 hours of job completion.

How quickly can I start using the platform?

Customers can start booking immediately after registration. Service providers typically get approved within 24-48 hours after document verification.

What support is available?

We provide 24/7 customer support via phone, email, and live chat. Our team is always ready to help with any questions or issues.

Is there a mobile app?

Yes! Our mobile apps for iOS and Android allow you to manage bookings, communicate with providers, and track services on the go.
