Frequently Asked Questions

How do I book a service?

Simply browse our service categories, select a provider, choose your preferred date and time, and complete the booking. You'll receive instant confirmation and can track your service request in real-time.

Are service providers verified?

Yes! All our service providers undergo thorough background checks, license verification, and skill assessments. We also verify insurance coverage and customer reviews before approving any provider.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards, debit cards, PayPal, and bank transfers. Payments are processed securely through our encrypted payment system, and you only pay after the service is completed to your satisfaction.

What is your cancellation policy?

You can cancel or reschedule your booking up to 24 hours before the scheduled service time without any charges. For cancellations within 24 hours, a small cancellation fee may apply.

How can I contact customer support?

Our customer support team is available 24/7 via phone, email, or live chat. You can also reach out through this contact form, and we'll respond within 2 hours during business hours.

How does the rating system work?

After each service, both customers and providers can rate each other on a 5-star scale. These ratings help maintain quality standards and help other users make informed decisions when choosing service providers.
